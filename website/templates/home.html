Welcome to Miss Mango's Class!



Miss Mango's class is a place where learning meets fun in the most engaging way possible! Step into our virtual classroom where super fun games await elementary school children eager to explore, discover, and learn. Here, you can take your learning to next levels with our progress AI technology that helps create tailored questions for your students. With our innovative approach, education transforms into an adventure, making every lesson an enjoyable journey of knowledge and discovery. Join us as we embark on a quest to make learning not just effective, but also incredibly fun!




Every student has different strengths, and Miss Mango’s class aims to use those strengths to make learning the most fun it can be! Miss Mango's classroom uses AI to turn the content of your lesson into different ways for your student to learn. Depending on your student, we have visual, auditory, kinesthetic, and regular text-based options! If your student doesn’t know what type of learning style they favor, we use AI to track your students' retention of information and help determine the best style for them.
